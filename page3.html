What You Can Do To Help

There are many things you can do to reduce your carbon footprint. Some of these things are:
1. Bike, Walk, Carpool or use Public Transport
2. Eat less Red Meat & Dairy
3. Buy locally produced food or grow your own food
4. Use reusable items instead of single use ones
5. Reduce your energy use and switch to renewable energy
