What You Can Do To Help

There are many things you can do to reduce your carbon footprint. Some of these things are:
  • Bike, Walk, Carpool or use Public Transport
  • Eat less Red Meat & Dairy
  • Buy locally produced food or grow your own food
  • Use reusable items instead of single use ones
  • Reduce your energy use and switch to renewable energy
  • Recycle everything possible
